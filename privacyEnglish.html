User Privacy Terms




Protecting the user's personal information is a basic principle of Tu Mi (ToMe). The software does not collect the user's personal information. Photographs taken by or saved in the software will not be synced to the cloud and will be saved locally on the phone.


Without your consent, Tu Mi (ToMe) will not disclose any of your information to any company, organization or individual other than Tu Mi (ToMe), except as otherwise provided by laws and regulations.
